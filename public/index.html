The Problem

Proof of access shouldn’t require proof of everything.

Organizers need real eligibility checks, but direct wallet lookups expose balances and screenshots cannot be trusted. Events need a neutral layer between private proof and public receipt.

Wallet privacy is fragile Screenshots are unverifiable Receipts are hard to audit

What I Built

Private gates for
high-signal events.

VeriGate lets organizers create private RSVP gates, attendees prove eligibility with zkTLS, and reviewers inspect the receipt trail through third-party records.

AI compiles the policy. zkTLS proves eligibility. The verifier decides.

zkTLS eligibility proofs 0G audit memory KeeperHub pass execution ENS event identity GateAgent iNFT on 0G

How It Works

From private proof to inspectable receipt.

  1. 01Organizer creates a private gate with 0G Compute.
  2. 02Attendee proves ETH-holder eligibility with zkTLS.
  3. 03A deterministic verifier — not AI — approves the redacted proof.
  4. 040G Storage anchors the audit trail and proof metadata.
  5. 05KeeperHub mints the RSVP pass to a fresh recipient.
  6. 06ENS and GateAgent make the event inspectable and portable.
